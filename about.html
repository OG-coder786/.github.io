Cricut Story

My Cricut journey began with my growing interest in graphic design and my curiosity for custom-made projects. After exploring tutorials and learning resources, I purchased a Cricut machine and began experimenting with simple designs. With guidance from my professors at George Brown College, I continue to develop my skills and refine my design process.

What I Create

I create custom Cricut projects such as personalized gifts, vinyl decals, apparel, packaging elements, and heat transfer designs, while exploring branding and visual presentation.
